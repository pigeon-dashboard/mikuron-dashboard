How this works:
1. Vendor sends an invoice (PDF/image, scanned or e-invoice) to mptmanufacturing@gmail.com or mptadmin2020@gmail.com
2. Both mailboxes forward a copy to dominick.han@mikuronpteltd.com, which scans automatically every 15 minutes — reads both read and unread emails, marks them as read once processed
3. Claude extracts vendor name, invoice no, dates, amounts, and line items (Item Code, Part No, Dwg No, Lot No, Qty) — saved to the "Vendor Invoices" Google Sheet, one row per line item
4. Click Refresh above to pull the latest rows from the Sheet immediately, without waiting for the next page load
5. Rows marked Review Required mean extraction failed or was incomplete — open the PDF and fill in the sheet manually
6. If the same vendor + invoice number is found twice (e.g. forwarded in duplicate), the second copy is highlighted amber in the Sheet but not shown here — only the first one is published to this tab
